About Us


Located in the heart of Surrey, Starlinx Visa Services Ltd. is the most trusted name for all your immigration related needs. Our dedicated team of registered immigration consultants is committed to working diligently to make your immigration journey as smooth as possible. We have several years of experience working with our clients on a wide range of immigration paths. We pride ourselves in assisting our clients from the beginning of their Canadian journey to getting permanent residence and citizenship in Canada.


Our Team:

Starlinx Visa Services is a team of skilled immigration professionals specializing in both federal and provincial immigration programs. Our team is led by Anjali Gautam, founder and Director, Regulated Immigration Consultant and a member in good standing with The College of Immigration and Citizenship Consultants (CICC).

Why Choose Us?

As one of the most respected immigration consulting firms in the Lower Mainland, we understand how complex and overwhelming the immigration process can be. Our goal is to take this burden off of your shoulders and do the heavy lifting for you in a transparent, skilled manner. Our team works tirelessly to understand the needs of our clients as well as keep watch on the changing requirements of the Canadian government, so that the best suited options can be presented to our clients. We constantly assess the needs of the Canadian labour market as well, and work closely with several businesses to ensure that their labour needs are met by hiring foreign workers with the right credentials. At every stage of your immigration process, Starlinx will constantly support you because we genuinely care for our clients and we firmly believe that the only path to success is by truly caring for our clients.
